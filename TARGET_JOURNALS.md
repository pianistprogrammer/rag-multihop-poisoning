## 📝 Paper Positioning for Artificial Intelligence Review

### Suggested Title:
> **"Adversarial Attacks on Retrieval-Augmented Generation: A Comprehensive Study of Multi-Hop Reasoning Vulnerabilities and Defense Mechanisms"**

### Abstract Structure:
1. **Problem Statement**: RAG systems are increasingly deployed but vulnerable to poisoning attacks
2. **Research Gap**: No prior comprehensive study of multi-hop RAG poisoning
3. **Novel Contributions**:
   - Chain-of-Evidence attack with structured reasoning
   - First systematic study of multi-hop RAG poisoning
   - Comprehensive evaluation: 4 datasets × 4 attacks × 4 defenses × 3 poison levels
4. **Key Results**: 
   - Attack success rates across complexity levels
   - Defense effectiveness analysis
   - Multi-hop vs. single-hop vulnerability comparison
5. **Impact**: Critical insights for securing deployed RAG systems

### Key Selling Points:
- ✅ **Novel Attack Method**: Chain-of-Evidence (structured reasoning chains)
- ✅ **First Multi-hop Study**: No prior work on 2-hop and 4-hop RAG poisoning
- ✅ **Comprehensive Evaluation**: 240 experimental conditions
- ✅ **Practical Impact**: Real-world threat to deployed systems
- ✅ **Defense Analysis**: Systematic evaluation of 4 state-of-the-art defenses
- ✅ **Reproducible**: Complete open-source implementation with resume functionality
